## Modifier Keys

### Shift

- **Straight line mode**: Hold while drawing
- **Multi-select**: Hold while clicking frames in timeline

### Ctrl/Cmd

- **Undo/Redo**: Combined with Z/Y
- **Copy/Paste**: Combined with C/V/X
- **Multi-select**: Alternative to Shift for timeline

### Alt

- **Eyedropper**: Hold with brush to pick colors
- **Pan**: Alternative to middle-click for canvas panning

|---------|--------|

## Customizing Shortcuts

Currently, Celstomp uses fixed shortcuts. Customizable shortcuts may be added in future updates.

## Learning Shortcuts

To learn shortcuts efficiently:

1. **Start with essentials**: `?` (cheat sheet), `Space` (play), `E/R/Q/W` (navigation)
2. **Add tool shortcuts**: 1-8 for tool switching
3. **Practice drawing shortcuts**: `[`/`]` for size, `Shift` for straight lines
4. **Incorporate edit shortcuts**: Undo/Redo for workflow

## Efficiency Tips

- **Use number keys**: Instantly switch tools without clicking toolbar
- **Draw straight lines**: `Shift` + drag for precise lines
- **Quick color picking**: `Alt` + click instead of switching tools
- **Navigate quickly**: `E/R/Q/W` for faster frame movement
- **Play often**: `Space` to frequently review animation progress
